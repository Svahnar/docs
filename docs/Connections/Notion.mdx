# Notion

Connect SVAHNAR to your **Notion workspace** to give agents the ability to read and write pages and databases.

---

## What Agents Can Do

| Capability | Description |
|---|---|
| **Read Pages** | Retrieve the content of Notion pages |
| **Create Pages** | Create new pages in a workspace or database |
| **Update Pages** | Edit existing page content and properties |
| **Search** | Search across accessible pages and databases |

---

## Connecting Notion

<Steps>
<Step>

### Open Connections

Go to the **Connections** page in the SVAHNAR Platform.

</Step>
<Step>

### Connect Notion

Click **Connect** on the **Notion** provider card.

A Notion authorization popup opens.

</Step>
<Step>

### Select Pages to Share

Notion asks you to select **which pages** SVAHNAR can access. You can choose individual pages, entire sections, or all pages in your workspace.

:::tip Share what you need
You can always expand or restrict which pages SVAHNAR can access by going to **Settings → My connections** in Notion and updating the page selection for SVAHNAR.
:::

Click **Allow access**.

</Step>
<Step>

### Confirm Connection

The popup closes. The **Notion** card updates to **Connected**.

</Step>
</Steps>

## Using Notion in an Agent

```yaml title="agent.yaml" showLineNumbers
create_agent_network:
  agent-1:
    agent_name: "Notion_Assistant"
    LLM_config:
      params:
        model: "gpt-5"
    tools:
      tool_assigned:
        - name: Notion
    agent_function:
      - "Search the user's Notion workspace and help them find and update pages."
    incoming_edge:
      - Start
    outgoing_edge: []
```

For detailed tool configuration, see the [Notion Tool](/docs/Tools/ToolList/Notion) reference.

---

## Token Behavior

Notion tokens **do not expire** — once connected, you remain connected indefinitely until you explicitly disconnect.

---

## Disconnect

Click **Disconnect** on the Notion card. SVAHNAR removes the stored token.

You can also revoke access directly from your Notion workspace under **Settings → My connections**.

---

## Troubleshooting

**"Page not found" or "Insufficient permissions"**
> The page you are trying to access may not have been shared with SVAHNAR. In Notion, go to **Settings → My connections → SVAHNAR** and add the relevant pages to the shared list.
